a running list of links
I like making lists- my favourite being my list of cool links I find on the Internet. I thought it would be nice to more formally synthesize my thoughts and document my favourite articles, products, resources- the list goes on.
Lofi Player
I listen to Lofi religiously when designing, but that the music was the reason why I added this link to the list. By interacting with elements in the room, you can build your own custom music room and share your unique room with others. Great aesthetic and kept me amused for an embarrassingly long amount of time.
The Structure of Stand Up Comedy
This website dissects Ali Wong’s stand-up comedy special, Baby Cobra. I have a newfound respect for stand up comedians- the thoughtfulness and strategy applied to every line in an hour-long production is commendable to say the least. I've never seen data visualiztion and animation done better.
The Magic iPod
2000s music has a special place in my heart. This website has a selection of around 50 hit tracks from the 2007 and mashes them together for you. An honourable mention would be Touch The Sky x Island In The Sun.
Wait But Why: How to Pick a Career (That Actually Fits You)
I love Tim Urban. In his inimitable style, he tackles the most enigmatic, entangled topics, ranging from AI to politics, from cryonics to picking a life partner. Simplifying the complex for the curious, Tim Urban distills and presents complex ideas in a way that resonants with others. This is one of my favourite articles of his.

What I love of about this article- and all of Urbans writing for that matter is his unique style; his combination of wry stick-figure illustrations and epic prose. "I’m not going to cater to anyone. I’m going to write a post that I wish someone would send me.”
Shortcuts
Shortcuts are great, this is a great resource that has made me more efficient in my design, development, and everything in between.
The Social Dilemma
I loved this documentary - thought provoking and hair raising.
